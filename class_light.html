class  Attenuation
 Describes the light falloff behavior. Meaning its intensity depending on the distance of the lighten fragment and the light source. This being

f = 1 / (1 + c + (l * d) + (q * d * d))

where f is the falloff, c the Constant value, l the Linear value, q the Quadratic value and d the distance. More...
